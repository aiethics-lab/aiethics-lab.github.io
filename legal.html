description Disclaimer

This AI Ethics Toolkit is an educational resource developed for the Computer Science Program at Muhlenberg College. It is intended for academic and demonstration purposes only.

The tools provided herein simulate various AI behaviors and ethical dilemmas. They should not be used for making critical decisions in production environments or for assessing real-world risk without professional consultation.

While we strive for accuracy, the algorithms and data simplified for educational clarity may not reflect the full complexity of state-of-the-art production systems.

privacy_tip Data Privacy

Client-Side Processing: This toolkit is designed to run entirely in your web browser. No data you upload (e.g., CSV files for the Bias Auditor) is sent to any external server. All processing happens locally on your device.

Local Storage: We use your browser's Local Storage to save your preferences (such as dark mode settings). This data remains on your device and is not tracked by us.
